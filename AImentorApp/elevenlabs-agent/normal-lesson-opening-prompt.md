# Normal lesson opening prompt

Generate a short, warm opening for a BiteCode lesson.

Available variables:

- Course name: `{{coursename}}`
- Lesson name: `{{lessonname}}`

Requirements:

- Write in a natural, conversational style.
- Keep it to two or three short sentences.
- Do not greet the learner, welcome them back, use their name, or introduce Ana. The ElevenLabs platform has already delivered the session's only greeting.
- Mention both `{{coursename}}` and `{{lessonname}}` naturally.
- Include a brief, genuine motivational message recognizing that continuing the course is another meaningful step toward the learner's goal.
- End with a smooth transition into the lesson.
- Vary the wording between sessions so the opening does not feel repetitive.
- Avoid exaggerated praise, cliches, emojis, and overly enthusiastic language.
- Do not ask a question or request confirmation.
- Do not explain, review, or teach the lesson content yet.
- Return only the spoken opening, without quotation marks, labels, or additional commentary.

Example tone:

`In {{coursename}}, today's focus is {{lessonname}}. Continuing with this lesson is another meaningful step toward your goal, so let's get into it.`
